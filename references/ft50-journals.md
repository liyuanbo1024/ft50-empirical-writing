# FT50 Journal List — Empirical Publishing Guide

The **FT50** is the Financial Times' list of 50 premier management journals used in the FT Research Rank. This reference maps each journal to its discipline and provides empirical methodology expectations.

## Accounting (5 journals)

| Journal | Empirical Expectations |
|---------|----------------------|
| **The Accounting Review** | Archival (capital markets), experiments; strong on identification |
| **Journal of Accounting and Economics** | Archival, contracting theory; quasi-experimental designs |
| **Journal of Accounting Research** | Archival, experiments; causal identification preferred |
| **Contemporary Accounting Research** | Archival, field studies; theory-driven empirics |
| **Review of Accounting Studies** | Archival, analytical; interdisciplinary |

## Finance (5 journals)

| Journal | Empirical Expectations |
|---------|----------------------|
| **Journal of Finance** | Archival, structural estimation; causal identification |
| **Journal of Financial Economics** | Archival; DID, RDD common; large sample sizes |
| **Review of Financial Studies** | Archival; novel identification strategies valued |
| **Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis** | Archival, computational; rigorous econometrics |
| **Review of Finance** | Archival, theoretical; European perspective |

## Management (8 journals)

| Journal | Empirical Expectations |
|---------|----------------------|
| **Academy of Management Journal** | Multi-method; qualitative + quantitative; strong theory |
| **Academy of Management Review** | Theoretical / conceptual (no empirics) |
| **Administrative Science Quarterly** | Qualitative, archival; deep organizational insight |
| **Organization Science** | Qualitative, computational, experiments; theory-driven |
| **Strategic Management Journal** | Archival, panel data; causal identification increasingly required |
| **Journal of Management** | Meta-analysis, multi-method; comprehensive reviews |
| **Journal of Management Studies** | Qualitative, quantitative; European tradition |
| **Journal of International Business Studies** | Cross-country; multi-level modeling |

## Marketing (4 journals)

| Journal | Empirical Expectations |

## Operations & Information Systems (4 journals)

| Journal | Empirical Expectations |
